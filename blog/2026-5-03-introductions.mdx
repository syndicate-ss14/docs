Let's introduce ourselves.

We are the Syndicate, and we operate Macrocosm. Starting today, we are opening the doors to more directly communicate with contributors, and anyone else curious, through our Discord server.

# the Syndicate

The name is accurate: we are a [syndicate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syndicate). We are a collaboration of like-minded Space Station 14 communities — presently The Den, Funky Station, and impstation — who have decided to join forces and share resources to build the best SS14 that we can.

We do not have a leader. We do not have a project owner. It's not relevant who started the Syndicate, or who has the most seniority. We are all equal, and we operate on [consensus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Consensus_decision-making).

Our current and biggest project is Macrocosm.

# Macrocosm

Consider Macrocosm to be a "midstream," or curated downstream. The resources of our member communities get applied here, to create a base SS14 we can be proud of and can happily utilize as downstreams.

The primary tenents of Macrocosm are as follows:

1. **Consensus.** Every project pull requested to Macrocosm is subject to internal vote. We do not merge anything if a member community objects to it. Contributors and players of those communities can feel confident that their servers will keep their uncompromised identity, and non-member communities who choose to fork Macrocosm will never have the rug pulled out from under them with an unexpected, paradigm-changing shift to the game.
2. **Consistency.** We only merge projects that have been throughly playtested by a downstream. We try to keep the highest code quality possible, and wish to avoid bug-fixing and microbalance pull requests at all costs. We do not want to introduce problems, only solve them.
3. **Customizability.** We place an extremely high priority on the ability for Macrocosm features to be easily enabled or disabled by our downstreams. We want setting up a Macrocosm server to feel like browsing a curated mod list, where you can explore many unique choices to make your server match your ideal experience.
