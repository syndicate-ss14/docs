# Monetization Policy
> “It has left remaining no other nexus between man and man than naked self-interest, than callous ‘cash payment.’”
> — Marx, Karl, and Friedrich Engels. The Communist Manifesto. 1848.

## Macrocosm's Stance on Monetization

Macrocosm as an upstream will neither be monetized nor permit any form of direct monetization ever.

For clarity, forms of monetization include:
- Patreons/Crowdfunding
- Code bounties
- Salaries for maintainers
- Any form of merchandizing
- Bribery/lobbying

Microcosms however are free to pursue their own forms of monetization within their own servers as long as it does not directly impact the Macrocosm as a whole.

## Policy on Code Bounties and Commissioned Content

Discussion of code bounties is confined to each microcosm’s own servers. While resulting work may be upstreamed per committee, bounties must not be proposed or justified for content that is primarily intended for the macrocosm.
