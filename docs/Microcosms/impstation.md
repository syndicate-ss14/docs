# IMPstation


<div class="text--center">
  ![impstation logo](/img/impstation.png)
</div>

IMPstation is a private, invite-only MRP server for fans of [RTVS](https://radiotv.solutions). Spun off of the community of twitch streamer Mirakurutaimu, IMP has had an outsized impact on the SS14 community.

IMPstation is known as the originating codebase for [Cosmic Cult](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZM2cE20K6w8), [Replicators](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oOzmoUJs0_I), and [Thaven](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nazzUM3HEGQ).
